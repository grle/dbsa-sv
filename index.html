Who We Are

DBSA Silicon Valley is a chapter of the Depression & Bipolar Support Alliance, which is dedicated to helping people with mood disorders. We are a group of volunteers located in the Bay Area and we provide support groups for those who are struggling with their mental illnesses.

Everyone is welcome here. Feel free to drop by and let us know how we can help you! You are not alone in this struggle and we are here to support your recovery.

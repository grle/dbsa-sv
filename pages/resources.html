Resources

If you are having thoughts of death or suicide, call 988 or text DBSA to 741-741.
If you need immediate assistance, please go to the nearest hospital emergency room.

Emergency

If you are in a crisis, reach out to the following numbers:

  • National Suicide Prevention Hotline: Call or text 988
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • The Trevor Project for LGBT Youth: Call 866-488-7386 / Text START to 678678
  • DeafLEAD Crisis Line: Call 321-800-DEAF (3323) / Text HAND to 839863

WRAP Model

Wellness Recovery Action Plan (WRAP) is an action plan that lets you take control of your mental health.

With WRAP, you can:

  • Identify tools that work for your mental health
  • Develop a plan to stay on track with your life and wellness goals
  • Identify warning signs and what to do when things start going off track
  • Outline how you want people to support you in the event of a mental health crisis

Get more information from the official website or download the app on Apple Store or Google Play.
